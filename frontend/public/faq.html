Frequently Asked Questions

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept Visa, and MasterCard.

How do you calculate the number of days in a rental?

We count each day that you have the item as one day. We do not double book days because we need time to inspect and clean the items to our very high standards.

Is there a minimum rental period?

Yes. As a general rule, we have a 2-day minimum rental period. However, Providers can decide to offer one day rentals for certain situations, such as party rentals. If you are interested in a 1 or 2 day rental, please reach out to the Provider to see if they can accommodate your request.

How do order cancellation work?

If a Provider cancels or removes a portion of your reservation for any reason the service fee will be returned to you.
If you cancel a reservation, the service fee is non-refundable. If you choose to remove a portion of your reservation, the service fee is non-refundable.

Where do you deliver your items?

As a newly released platform, we only deliver our items to the downtown Toronto. Our Independent Providers deliver and set up clean, safe and insured items to private residences, vacation rentals, hotels and even airports. Once you complete your order your Provider will be in contact to arrange specific delivery details.

How much does delivery cost?

Each Independent Provider sets their own delivery rates and the locations they deliver to. There may be extra fees for same day delivery, delivery outside of normal business hours, or on holidays. Please check with the Provider you would like to rent from to get the most accurate delivery costs.

What is the Rentitus service fee?

To help cover the costs of running Rentitus including processing fees, customer support, and our Trust and Safety program, we charge customers a service fee between 7% and 9% every time a reservation is made through our platform. The amount of this service fee is based on the reservation subtotal of rental and delivery charges (before other fees and taxes). The exact amount of the service fee is displayed before a customer pays for their reservation and is also displayed in the cart, delivery information, billing information and payment pages.
